WHO AM I?

ABOUT ME

Hi there! My name is Chelsey Bellacicco, and I’m currently a first year Interactive Media Developer student at Camosun College looking to pursue a career in game design and/or design in general. I’ve realized that I'm passionate about design so I’m quite eager to gain more industry experience and work alongisde other passionate individuals. I'm from Windsor, Ontario originally but have lived in the beautiful city of Victoria for over 6 years. In my spare time I enjoy playing board games, going on nature walks with my partner, drawing, and gaming.

I made the decision to enroll into the IMD program at Camosun to reignite that creative spark I thought I had lost, and to make a major career change. I know I still have a long road ahead but I didn't want to live with any regrets. Growing up, I loved creating things whether it was drawings, writing stories or editing music videos on Adobe Premiere. When I was 12 years old, I joined a video game day camp where I designed my 5 levels with 2 other kids on the Neverwinter Nights module mode. In high school I would also code mini websites using HTML or make my own art in Adobe Photoshop.

Midterm Website Project

Typography Cover

I designed a book cover with the main focus on typography. The cover is for a story I had come up with years ago and may revisit for a possible future game. This was created using both Procrate and Canva.

I'm quite happy with how this turned out overall and hope to create more zines. Kingdom Hearts belongs to Disney and Square Enix.

Website Lab Assignment

Mind-Map Project

I designed a Mind-Map in my Design Thinking course as a first major project. This whole mind map was done using only Adobe InDesign with some icons from google fonts. The Mind-Map was to focus on on a problem definition, and ideas on how to improve on the Interurban cafeteria.
